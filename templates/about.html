About Us.

Dhi Darpan is a blog that explores the intersection of philosophy and technology, as well as the creative potential of fictional writing. Dhi Darpan means "Mind Mirror" in Sanskrit, and the blog aims to reflet on the various aspects of human existance. such as ethics, values, logic, consiousness, and creativity. The blog also showcases original stories and essays that are inspired by philosophical themes or technological innovations. This is a platform for sharing opinions and thoughts on topics that matter to the modern world, and for engaging with curious minds who are interested in learning and growing.

Welcome to dhi darpan,

Well, the first thing you might want to know is what does Dhi Darpan mean?

These are Sanskrit words where Dhi means 'Thoughts' and Darpan means 'Mirror', So roughly Dhi Darpan translates to 'Mirror of Thoughts'.

Dhis is not a company nor an organization this is just an individual project.
